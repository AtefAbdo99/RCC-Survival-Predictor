Welcome to RCC Survival Predictor

This application predicts the overall survival (OS) in months for patients with Renal Cell Carcinoma (RCC) based on various clinical variables.

Our model is trained on historical RCC patient data using advanced machine learning techniques including Random Forest and Gradient Boosting.

About RCC Survival Predictor

RCC Survival Predictor is a cutting-edge tool designed to assist oncologists and healthcare professionals in estimating overall survival rates for patients with Renal Cell Carcinoma.

By leveraging machine learning algorithms, we provide accurate predictions based on a comprehensive set of clinical variables specific to RCC.
